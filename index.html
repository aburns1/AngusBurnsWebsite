Angus Burns

Hello, I am Angus Burns. Welcome to my website.

Where do I Call Home

I grew up in British Columbia in a small town called Slocan. It is located in the West Kootenays. It is equidistant between three beautiful ski hills. In the summer I enjoyed wakeboarding on Slocan Lake, and hike in the surrounding mountains.

Since I have enrolled as a student in the faculty of engineering at Carleton University, I have lived in Ottawa and it has become my new home.

Little Bit About School

I am a student in Computer Systems Engineering at Carleton University. I have been living in Ottawa since 2009. My courses have focused on realtime microprocessor systems, digital logic devices, and analog electronics.

I am currently working on my fourth year project with the goal of creating a wearable electronic assistive device for visually impaired swimmers.

What I Enjoy in Ottawa

I enjoy cooking. Going grocery shopping is enjoyable for me. I longboard as one of my main modes of transportation. It is a quick way around the city and is easy to take on the bus with me or store in the coat check of a local establishment while I walk around downtown.
